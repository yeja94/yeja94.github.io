World Cities Ranked By Annual Sunshine Hours

Objective

To find out which cities get hit the most by the sun? Or What Is the Best City to Sing "You Are My Sunshine"? Do people who lives in these cities/country really feel happy?

Datasets

Dataset by Makeover Monday Week 44, 2019. The csv file was directly downloaded from data.world. Created by @evamurray, the dataset was clean and reable in 16 columns.

Strategy

As usual, I have chosen Tableau, because it is flexible and easy to use for any data visualization. I filtered out the structure data by cities and country with the yearly sunshine hours. Having a bar graphs would display the top or least sunshine hours easily. I decided to chose a red scale color for sunshines. I wanted to know which part of the world get the most sunshines. Taking the overall measurement of yearly sunshines in every city in the world, I plotted them in a Maps chart to display a world map of each city. In addition, each location display a density of sunshine hours which show a better visualization.
